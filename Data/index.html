Poverty and Race in Los Angeles

A simple Tableau visualization analyzing the correlation between poverty rates and proportion of the population that are people of color in Los Angeles County census block sections. Using geospatial data, I then plotted these results on an interactive map. Data obtained from US Census Bureau. Thumbnail Image by me.
